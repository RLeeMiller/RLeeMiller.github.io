Player Profile: Dwayne Haskins

Class: RS Sophomore

Height: 6-3 Weight: 220 lbs.

Hometown: Potomac, Maryland

The young quarterback from Maryland has performed admirably in his first season as a starter, garnering Heisman hype and leading the talented Buckeye squad to a 6-0 record. "Simba" is certainly a fitting nickname for this prolific passer. While he may be young, he has already proven himself in difficult situations at the Big House (in relief of JT Barrett), at TCU, and at Penn State. The sky is the limit for this Buckeye offense with Haskins at the helm!

Yes, it appears that this star was destined for the throne from a young age!
