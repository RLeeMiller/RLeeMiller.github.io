The Buckeye State

Founded in 1870, The Ohio State University has grown to become the 3rd largest campus in the United States, boasting over 66,000 students across several campuses. There are nearly 500,000 living alumni across the world, so no matter where you may be, a loud "O-H" is likely to get an "I-O!!!" in response!!!

Ohio boasts a beautiful seal, not unlike this website's design! The seal features a sunrise in Chillicothe, Ohio, the state's original capital, along with wheat and arrows. Astute onlookers notice the sun's 13 rays representing the original colonies, as well as 17 arrows in the sheath as Ohio is the 17th state.
